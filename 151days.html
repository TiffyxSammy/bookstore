Overview

In this course you will study the principles of Software Engineering, focusing on Object-Oriented Software Engineering. We will begin with the introductory discussion of the software development process and what constitutes well-engineered software. Then we will move on to software specification and requirements definition. The next part of the course will be devoted to software design. Although we will discuss some of the major design techniques, we will specifically concentrate on Object-Oriented Design (OOD). A significant portion of the course will be dedicated to learning the Unified Modeling Language (UML). :)
